Apple Crisp

A warm, comforting apple crisp with a crunchy oat topping — perfect with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.

Ingredients

  • 4 apples, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix apples with cinnamon and sugar.
  3. Make topping with oats, brown sugar, and butter.
  4. Bake 35-40 minutes until golden.
